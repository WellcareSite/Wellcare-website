## The Short Answer: Yes

If your child has a developmental delay, autism diagnosis, or other qualifying condition, **Health First Colorado (Medicaid) covers pediatric therapy services** including ABA, speech therapy, occupational therapy, and physical therapy.

At WellCare & Nurture, we accept Health First Colorado and Colorado Access — and we handle the insurance verification for you so you can focus on your child.

## What Services Does Medicaid Cover?

Health First Colorado covers medically necessary pediatric therapy services, including:

| Service | What It Covers |
|---------|---------------|
| **ABA Therapy** | Behavioral support for children with autism spectrum disorder |
| **Speech Therapy** | Language delays, articulation, AAC devices, feeding therapy |
| **Occupational Therapy** | Sensory processing, fine motor skills, self-care, handwriting |
| **Physical Therapy** | Gross motor delays, balance, coordination, strength building |

Coverage is based on **medical necessity** — meaning your child's pediatrician or a qualified provider determines that therapy is needed for your child's development.

## Do I Need a Referral?

In most cases, **yes**. Health First Colorado typically requires a referral from your child's primary care provider (pediatrician). Here's how the process usually works:

1. **Talk to your pediatrician** about your concerns
2. **Get a referral** for a therapy evaluation
3. **Call us** — we'll verify your child's benefits and schedule the evaluation
4. **Start therapy** — once the evaluation confirms a need, we develop a treatment plan

If you're not sure where to start, we can help guide you through the process. Many families are surprised at how straightforward it is.

## What About TRICARE?

For military families in Colorado Springs, **TRICARE covers pediatric therapy services** as well. Coverage details vary by plan (TRICARE Prime, Select, etc.), but most plans cover:

- Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) for autism
- Speech-language pathology
- Occupational therapy
- Physical therapy

We're an in-network TRICARE provider, which means lower out-of-pocket costs for your family.

## What If My Child Doesn't Have Insurance?

We believe every child deserves access to quality care. If you're uninsured or underinsured:

- **Colorado's [CHP+](https://www.healthfirstcolorado.com/) program** provides coverage for children in families who earn too much for Medicaid but can't afford private insurance
- **Early intervention programs** through the state may provide services at no cost for children under 3
- We can help connect you with community resources

## The Biggest Mistake Parents Make

Waiting. Many families assume therapy is too expensive or that insurance won't cover it — and they delay getting help during the most critical window of their child's development.

The reality is that **most families pay little to nothing out of pocket** when using Medicaid or TRICARE for pediatric therapy. And early intervention consistently leads to better outcomes.
